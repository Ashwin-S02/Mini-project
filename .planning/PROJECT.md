# Project: Maps Lead Scraper

## Vision
A powerful lead generation tool for a CRM company to identify businesses in need of digital transformation services (Websites, Social Media, WhatsApp Agents, Automation).

## Core Principles
- **Accuracy**: Reliable data extraction from Google Maps and business websites.
- **Efficiency**: Multi-threaded processing for fast lead gathering.
- **Actionability**: Data formatted directly for CRM use in Google Sheets.

## Target Audience
Sales and marketing teams looking for high-intent business leads.
